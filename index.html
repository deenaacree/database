Weatherbase Project: North Central Florida Weather Data

Welcome to The Weatherbase Project. This is a project created by a student at the University of Florida to log weather temperatures throughout various parts of North Central Florida.

As part of this information-gathering project, you can view the previous weather data that has been entered by others, enter more weather data, and edit or delete data that is not correct.

This data can be used by people all over the North Central Florida area in order to learn more about their location's average weather.
